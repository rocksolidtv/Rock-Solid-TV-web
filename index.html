About Me

My name is Rocky Smith, and I founded Rock Solid TV for one reason: truth belongs in the open. I didn’t build this to play nice with the mainstream. I built it because the Bible is being watered down, history is being rewritten, and free speech is treated like a problem to be fixed. Here, we speak plainly — raw, real, and straight from the King James Bible.

If you follow my YouTube channel, you know how fragile “platforms” can be. One policy change and voices vanish. That’s why this site exists: as our fallback fortress. If I’m ever banned or throttled elsewhere, this is where we regroup. Bookmark it. Share it. This is the place my subscribers can always keep in touch and keep getting the goods without gatekeepers.

What happens here? We open the Scriptures the modern church won’t touch: the hard verses, the prophecies unfolding, the calls to repent and stand. We also pull the covers off hidden history — the buried facts, the pattern-makers, the motives behind the messaging. Then we hand out Red Pills: not feel‑good slogans, but evidence‑packed breakdowns that help you see the machine and step out of it.

This site is more than a backup — it’s a living archive and a war room. You’ll find episodes, notes, and a PDF Vault you can download and share. If the lights go out on the big platforms, Rock Solid TV doesn’t go dark. We keep publishing, we keep connecting, and we keep telling the truth — uncensored, unfiltered, and unshakable.
